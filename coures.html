LEARING

Learning is a lifelong process. At St.Shining Stars, we begin by instilling healthy learning habits during the formative years to set the foundation for a future rich with knowledge and purpose. As students continue to develop throughout their time at St. Shining Stars, we intentionally educate in the small moments and the big ones. We take these everyday opportunities to encourage our students to ask the harder questions, explore their interests, and learn through a lens of both faith and reason. From pre-kindergarten to eighth grade, students are supported and encouraged in every moment as they develop academically, physically, socially, and spiritually. Imagine St. Shining Stars education as a st aircase. Each year, students take a further step along their individual learning path. Every step our students take is supported by the foundation built on lessons learned in the years before and equips them with the resources necessary to succeed in the years that follow. Whether moving from pre-kindergarten to kindergarten or seventh grade to eighth grade, the year before serves as a solid foundation that allows our students to confidently step up to the next level and prepares them to face the new challenges ahead.
